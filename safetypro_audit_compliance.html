Risk Appetite Statement
INTOLERABLE: Must NOT proceed. Immediate halt. Engineering re-design or elimination required before work resumes.
HIGH: Work must not start until risk is reduced. Senior management approval required. Detailed control plan mandatory.
MEDIUM (ALARP): Acceptable only if further reduction is impracticable. Additional controls applied. Supervisor oversight required.
LOW: Broadly Acceptable. Manage by routine procedures. Monitor to ensure it remains low.
Likelihood & Severity Scales
Likelihood (L)
1 – Rare: Not expected to occur
2 – Unlikely: Could occur (yearly)
3 – Possible: Might occur (monthly)
4 – Likely: Will probably occur (weekly)
5 – Almost Certain: Expected regularly
Severity (S)
1 – Negligible: First aid only
2 – Minor: Medical treatment
3 – Moderate: LTI / restricted work
4 – Major: Permanent disability / fatality
5 – Catastrophic: Multiple fatalities
